Welcome to RelicRush

The ancient relic is yours... but the temple wants it back! A colossal boulder thunders in your wake, threatening to crush everything in its path. This isn't just a run for treasure—it's a high-speed dash for survival against an unstoppable force!

Relic Rush flips the classic runner genre on its head! Inspired by legendary boulder chase scenes, you're not just running away; you're facing the danger head-on. As the giant boulder closes in, you'll sprint towards the screen on a treacherous 3-lane track, forcing you to react in a split second. With intuitive swipe controls, you'll need lightning-fast reflexes to JUMP over gaping rivers, SLIDE under ancient blockades, and DODGE between crumbling walls.

INTENSE REVERSE-RUNNER ACTION: Experience the thrill of a chase from a unique "head-on" perspective that puts your reflexes to the ultimate test.

A WORLD OF DYNAMIC TRAPS: This is more than just a run! Slow down in treacherous mud, trip over hidden tree branches, and time your jumps perfectly to cross precarious moving platforms.

CUNNING ENEMIES: Watch out for the crafty hedgehogs! Some patrol the path, while others cleverly mimic your position, forcing you to think and act fast to get past them.

HUMOROUS CARTOON CHAOS: Inspired by classics like Crash Bandicoot, every failure is designed to be fun. Watch your character get comically flattened, deflate like a balloon, or see their shoes pop out of the water! (No blood, just pure cartoon fun).

STUNNING LOW-POLY WORLD: Immerse yourself in a vibrant and stylized low-poly temple on the brink of collapse.

COMPETE FOR GLORY: Climb the global leaderboards and prove to the world that you are the ultimate relic runner!

The boulder is rolling. Your adventure awaits. How long can YOU survive the chase?

Download Relic Rush now and test your limits!
